Jaen Oils

ALMAZARA ACEITES DE OLIVA DEL SUR

Company founded in 1986 with origins in the union of several professionals linked to the sector of olive oil, from farmers to manufacturers of machinery for oil mills. All of them, moved by a great passion for this product millennium, Extra Virgin Olive Oil shaped this oil mill, which was established in the area of ​​old orchards in the province of Jerez, which gave rise to the brand under which our oil is marketed, "EL VILLAR DE JAEN".
For Aceites de Jerez, the farmer is one of the most important pieces, since we are aware of the constant effort that must be made to produce a healthy fruit that, when harvested at its optimal point of maturation, allows us to obtain a top quality oil that is capable of satisfying the most demanding palates as well as the needs of the Consumers are increasingly more concerned about a healthy diet.

In our olive oil factory, we have a great team of professionals who oversee the entire process and traceability of the product, from the field to the consumer. We have been dedicated to olive cultivation for several generations, including farmers, small producers, technicians, and sales professionals. Additionally, we have a specialized team in foreign trade..

Facilities
  • Stainless steel tank with a capacity of 15,000 and 40,000 kg
  • Climate-controlled cellar with food-grade nitrogen injection to enhance fruit preservation
  • Packaging machine with three packaging lines, automated, and with air conditioning in all its areas.
  • Certified for the storage and packaging of virgin olive oil Kosher y Kosher de Pascua
  • Storage room with shelves
  • Quality certificate ISO-9001 e ISO-22000
